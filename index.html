About Us

Currency X is a commercial website, my submission for Assignment Three Interactive Media. Assignment Three is required to have two data visualizations using data from an API. I am using the Fixer API, which is for current and historical foreign exchange (forex) rates. Along with this, we had to focus on the design and theory aspects while designing the website.
